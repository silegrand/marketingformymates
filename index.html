01 — The Problem

Your Bigger Competitors Are Drowning You Out. And You Probably Know It.

Most skilled trades have a basic website. A Facebook page that hasn't been touched in six months. Maybe a Google listing with three reviews from 2019. That used to be fine. It isn't anymore.

[ERR_001]

You're invisible where it counts

When a facilities manager searches for a CCTV installer in your area, they're not flicking through leaflets. They're Googling. And the businesses that show up consistently are the ones with active, well-optimised digital presences — not necessarily the best operators.

[ERR_002]

Large competitors are outspending you massively

Regional and national players spend thousands a month on SEO, content, and ads. Not because they're better at the job but because they know that visibility wins contracts before price or quality even gets a look-in. Small businesses lose work they should have won.

[ERR_003]

Your customers want local. They just can't find you.

Plenty of buyers actively prefer a local, reliable provider. They want someone who'll pick up the phone, knows the area, and won't disappear after the install. But if your digital footprint is weak, those customers go with whoever they can actually find.

The good news? You don't need to match their budget. You need consistent, targeted marketing that works in the background while you get on with the job. Regular content, a professional web presence, and basic SEO done properly will outperform most of what your local competitors are doing — and put you in front of the customers already looking for exactly what you offer.
